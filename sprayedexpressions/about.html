About Sprayed Expressions

On Christmas of 2011, I (Sivan) received the most thoughtful and creative gift! Josh had spray painted a portrait of he and myself, and our two furry children (Edison & Napoleon) on canvas. From there he went on to explore painting on different surfaces and textures. I too discovered I had a knack for the creative and the administrative; so we decided to share our craft with others and on one freaky Friday the 13th, Sprayed Expressions was born.

We love our planet, so we keep our artwork earth-friendly down to the very spray paint we use. We upcycle previously loved vinyl records, sheet music, and canvases too! We give them new life and prevent them from ending up in the landfills.

If you want to see more of our behind the scenes photos, or our interview with FOX 12 Portland, check out our Facebook page: www.facebook.com/sprayedexpressions
